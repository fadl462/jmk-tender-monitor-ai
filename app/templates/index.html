About this platform

JMK Tender Monitor AI uses free, rule-based matching — not a paid language model — to find and score tenders and consultancy jobs against JMK's sectors of work. Ghana job platforms are always checked first; international platforms are only checked as a fallback. Treat results as a shortlist to review, not a guarantee — always confirm details on the original listing.
